# LAIR Research

**Project:** LAIR – Local AI Intelligence Router

**Version:** 0.1.0-alpha

**Status:** Active Research

---

# Purpose

The Research section contains exploratory work for LAIR.

Unlike architecture documents or ADRs, research documents describe ideas that are still under investigation.

Research does **not** represent accepted architecture.

Its purpose is to explore possibilities, evaluate alternatives, and document findings before major engineering decisions are made.

---

# Research Philosophy

Innovation should be intentional.

Every significant idea should pass through a structured research process before becoming part of the architecture.

Research allows experimentation without introducing instability into the core system.

---

# Research Lifecycle

Every research topic follows the same progression.

```
Innovation Backlog

↓

Research

↓

Prototype

↓

RFC

↓

ADR

↓

Implementation

↓

Benchmark

↓

Production
```

Research exists before architecture.

Architecture exists before implementation.

# Research Principles

Research should be:

- Evidence-based
- Reproducible
- Well documented
- Benchmark driven
- Independent of implementation

Research should encourage exploration while protecting the stability of the production architecture.

# Definition of Success

Research succeeds when it produces one of three outcomes:

1. The idea is adopted and becomes an RFC.

2. The idea is rejected with documented reasoning.

3. The research identifies new opportunities for future exploration.

Every research effort should produce knowledge, regardless of the outcome.
